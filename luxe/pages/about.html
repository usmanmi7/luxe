Our story

Formulated first.
Decorated second.

LUXE started with a simple complaint: most "clean beauty" looked beautiful and did nothing. So we flipped the order of operations.

2021 → today

Started in a rented lab, not a studio.

LUXE was founded in Colombo in 2021 by a chemist who was tired of seeing pretty packaging hide weak formulas. We spent our first eighteen months in a borrowed lab space before we shot a single product photo.

Today we work with an independent cosmetic chemistry lab to test every formula at clinical concentrations, then send it to real people for four to six weeks before it ever reaches a shelf.

What we won't compromise on

Three rules that apply to every formula, every time.

01

Active concentrations, not trace amounts

If an ingredient is on the label, it's there at a concentration that actually does something — not a marketing sprinkle.

02

Tested on people, not just on paper

Every formula goes through a real-world trial period with a panel of testers before it's approved for production.

03

Packaging that earns its footprint

Refillable where we can manage it, recyclable everywhere else. No packaging that exists purely to look expensive.
