Quick answers

Before you write in — these might save you a step.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 48 hours and arrive in 3–7 business days domestically, 7–14 internationally.

What's your return policy?

Unopened products can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Opened products are covered under our happiness guarantee — contact care@luxecosmetics.com.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes — every LUXE product is certified by Leaping Bunny and we never test on animals, anywhere in our supply chain.

Do you offer wholesale or stockist accounts?

We do. Select "Wholesale & partnerships" in the form above and our team will follow up within 3 business days.
